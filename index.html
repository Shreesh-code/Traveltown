    Why the pearl city can be your vacation

    Hyderabad is known as the City of Pearls due to its booming trade of pearls.Hyderabad is located in the southern part of Telangana in southeastern India , along the banks of the Musi river , a tributary of the Krishna River located on the Deccan Plateau in the northern part of South India.

    • The craft that is known to be the pride of Telangana is Bidri Craft. Today, Hyderabad accounts for the highest production of Bidriware in India. It is told that Iran migrants brought the art to the city. The Bidri Craft is used for making cigar, Huqqa buttons, jewelry boxes, flower vases and many more. Popular Bidri art gift articles that are very famous include cufflinks ,paper knives ,key chains and paperweights.
    • The culture of Hyderabad is also known as Hyderabad Tehzeeb or Dakini Tehzeeb which is the traditional cultural lifestyle of the Telugu people and the Hyderabadi Muslims.
    • The Hyderabadi cuisine is an amalgamation of South Asian, Mughalai, Turkic, and Arabic along with the influence of local Telangana and Marathwada cuisines. The delicious Biryani available here is probably one of the most famous things about Hyderabad city. The Nizams have a history of serving more than 26 varieties of biryani to their guests. Some of the most popular ones that stayed on even after ages are – Hyderabadi Chicken Dum Biryani, Lamb Biryani, Hyderabad Zafrani Biryani and Keeme ki khichdi. A visit to the Biryani paradise won’t be complete without a visit to the famous Paradise Biryani.

    Whatever you desire. Hyderabad provides.
